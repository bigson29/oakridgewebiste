📋 Server Rules
✕
SERVER RULES & GUIDELINES

All players must follow these rules. Failure to comply will result in warnings, kicks, or bans.

🎭 Roleplay First

  • Always stay in character
  • No OOC chat in-character
  • Value your character's life

⚖️ Fair Play

  • No RDM / No VDM
  • No metagaming
  • No powergaming

🚗 Traffic & Vehicles

  • Drive realistically
  • No GTA-style driving
  • Yield to emergency vehicles

🎤 Communication

  • Working mic required
  • No background noise
  • English only in public

💰 Economy & Crime

  • No exploiting
  • Realistic escalation
  • No cop baiting

👮 Respect Staff

  • Follow staff instructions
  • Appeals via Discord
  • Staff decisions are final
⚠️ By applying you agree to follow all rules.
